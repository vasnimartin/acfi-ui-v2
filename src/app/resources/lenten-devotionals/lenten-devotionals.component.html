Lenten Daily Devotionals 2024

A 40-day journey through Psalm 119

Rev. Dr. Samson Parekh

Pastor's Desk · Published February–March 2024

The Central Theme: Delighting in God's Word

"But his delight is in the law of the Lord, and on his law he meditates day and night. He is like a tree planted by streams of water..."

— Psalm 1:2–3

Throughout this 40-day Lenten journey, we have walked through the profound depths of Psalm 119, the longest chapter in the Bible and a magnificent meditation on God's Word. Each day has revealed new facets of how Scripture guides, sustains, and transforms us.

"Preach the word; be ready in season and out of season; reprove, rebuke, and exhort, with complete patience and teaching."

— 2 Timothy 4:2

As we conclude this devotional series, let us remember that God's Word is not merely to be read, but to be lived. It is our lamp and our light, our counselor and our strength.

Central Reflection

The Word of God is living and active, sharper than any two-edged sword. As we delight in His law and meditate on it day and night, we become like trees planted by streams of water—bearing fruit in season and prospering in all we do.

"Be strong, and show yourself a man, and keep the charge of the Lord your God, walking in his ways and keeping his statutes..."

— 1 Kings 2:2–3

"And by this we know that we have come to know him, if we keep his commandments."

— 1 John 2:3–5
