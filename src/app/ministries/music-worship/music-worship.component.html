Making a Joyful Noise

We are a group of amateurs who have come together to make a joyful noise unto the LORD. Our Lord does not give credit to talent, but He takes pride in HIS children—us—when we praise HIM with all our body, mind, and spirit.

We meet once every week before the worship service so we can get our act together. Our practice sessions are another opportunity for us to rejoice in the Lord.
