Outreach Ministry

“Sharing Christ’s love locally and globally.”

ACFI has identified Outreach as an integral part of worship. Our vision is to be a resource center for missions in India, help build churches, support ministries that help orphans, widows, the destitute, and strengthen local community outreach.

Local Outreach Ministry

We show the love of Christ to the elderly and destitute. Local outreach instills and imparts Christian identity by serving others. We serve at multiple nursing facilities within our community by visiting, singing, praying, and sharing God’s Word. Our children also participate joyfully.

We currently visit:

  • Pflugerville Care Center
  • Heatherwilde Assisted Living
  • Heartland Health Care Center

We plan to expand outreach through soup kitchens, AIDS Services of Austin, and Hill Country Community Center. Our goal is to reflect Christ’s love and help people experience the greatest gift—Christ Himself—in the greater Austin area.

PremMilan — University Student Ministry

Prem Milan is a group that fosters a sense of Indian community for undergraduate and graduate students from India and South Asia at UT Austin. We meet monthly at the Longhorn Baptist Student Center to discuss adjusting to U.S. life, scholarships, jobs, faith, music, social causes, and connections in Austin.

Meetings include free Indian meals, discussions, and occasional concerts such as Aradhna, helping students experience the richness of faith and culture.

Global Outreach Ministry

“Serving workers for the harvest.”

Serving Workers for the Harvest

Bro. Anupam Macky and Sis. Rachel support church leaders and Christian workers in Siliguri, Northeast India through church-based leadership training and discipleship.

Reaching Hand Society (RHS)

Founded by Dr. R.A.C. Paul and Dr. Iris Paul, RHS serves 926 villages across Odisha, working in health, education, prisoner rehabilitation, and tribal empowerment.

Promise Land Orphanage

Started by Rick and Radha White in 1997. Caring for abandoned children across two locations: Coimbatore and the Deborah Project. Focused on rescuing infants and providing Christian care.

Indian Evangelical Mission (IEM)

Founded in 1965. A faith mission that now supports 580 missionaries across India. ACFI supports Bro. Samuel and Sis. Gladys in Tamil Nadu.

Good News Baptist Church

Pastor Somaiah and Sis. Jaya Lakshmi left their jobs to serve a region once dominated by witchcraft and illiteracy. Today, over 32 villages have active house churches and trained leaders.

Compassion International

A global Christian organization releasing children from poverty in Jesus’ name. ACFI Sunday School supports Mahendra SelvaKumari.

Christian Evangelical Fellowship Mission (CEFM)

Founded by Apostle Selvaraj, sending missionaries into unreached villages of South India to preach the gospel.
