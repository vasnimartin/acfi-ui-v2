Children’s Ministry

Growing closer to God through His Word

Welcome to ACFI Kids

Our Children's Ministry is dedicated to partnering with parents to help children develop a growing relationship with Jesus Christ. We provide a safe, fun, and nurturing environment where children can learn Biblical truths and experience God's love.

Through engaging lessons, worship, and activities, we aim to build a strong spiritual foundation that will last a lifetime.

Sunday School Classes

Our Sunday School runs throughout the academic year, providing age-appropriate learning in a fun environment. Each week, children dive into Scripture, learn new songs, and participate in activities that reinforce the lesson.

Our Teaching Approach

  • Biblically grounded curriculum suitable for each age group.
  • Interactive learning through stories, crafts, and games.
  • Focus on Scripture memorization and application.
  • Encouraging personal prayer and worship.
  • Building healthy friendships within the church family.
