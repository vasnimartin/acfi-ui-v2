Requirements

Bachelor's degree in Theology or Divinity (Master's preferred)
Licensed or ordained minister
Minimum 10 years of total pastoral experience
Minimum 5 years as Senior Pastor of a congregation of 100+ members
Must relocate to Greater Austin, Texas area upon employment

Represent ACFI in community and church partnerships
