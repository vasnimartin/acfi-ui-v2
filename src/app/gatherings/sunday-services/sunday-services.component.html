Experience God with Us

Our Sunday worship is a time of refreshing, where we gather to lift up the name of Jesus through passionate praise, hear the transforming Word of God, and stand together in prayer. It’s a place where families grow and community is built.

We encourage in-person attendance to fully experience fellowship, but we warmly welcome our online family to join us live.

In-Person Service

  • Time Sundays, 10:00 AM
  • Location ACFI @ Stonehill SDA
    4301 Kelly Ln, Pflugerville, TX 78660
  • Parking & Kids Ample parking available. Nursery and Children's Church provided during service.

What to Expect

Dress Code: Come as you are, or in your Sunday best. We care about your heart, not your clothes.

Length: Services typically last about 90 minutes.

Atmosphere: Friendly, Spirit-filled, and family-oriented.
