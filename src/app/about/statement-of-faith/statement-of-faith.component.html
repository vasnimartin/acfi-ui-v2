Statement of Faith

What we believe according to Scripture.

We affirm the foundational truths of the Christian faith as taught in the Bible. These beliefs shape our worship, our mission, and our daily lives as followers of Christ.

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and the final authority for faith and life.

The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus Christ is fully God and fully man, whose life, death, and resurrection provide salvation for all who believe.

Salvation

We believe salvation is a gift of God, received by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone.

The Holy Spirit

We believe the Holy Spirit indwells believers, empowering them for holy living and spiritual service.

The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, discipleship, evangelism, and service.

Christian Living

We believe believers are called to pursue holiness, love one another, and live as witnesses of Christ.

The Return of Christ

We believe in the personal, visible return of Jesus Christ and the eternal hope of all believers.

Our Foundation

“All Scripture is God-breathed and useful for teaching, rebuking, correcting and training in righteousness.”

— 2 Timothy 3:16
