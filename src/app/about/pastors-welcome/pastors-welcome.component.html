Pastor’s Welcome

A message from our leadership.

Pastor John Daniel

Senior Pastor

Welcome to Austin Christian Fellowship of India!

We are grateful that you are here. Our prayer is that you experience God’s love, grow spiritually, and find meaningful community within our church family. We are a people saved by grace, and we exist to share that grace with everyone we meet.

At ACFI, we believe God has called each of us to live with purpose, hope, and compassion. Whether you have been following Christ for many years or are exploring faith for the first time, we want you to know that you are valued and loved.

Our church is built on prayer, worship, Scripture, fellowship, and service. We invite you to join us in growing deeper in our relationship with Christ and in sharing His love with our community.

We look forward to worshiping with you.

— Pastor John Daniel
